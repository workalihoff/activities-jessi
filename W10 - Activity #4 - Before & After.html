Part 3 — Voice Note
Talk it out
Choose 2 of the 3 prompts below and record yourself answering out loud — no script, no notes. 60–90 seconds total.
How to record & submit
1. Pick 2 of the 3 prompts below.
2. Think for 30 seconds — don't write anything.
3. Record your voice note on your phone (60–90 seconds total).
4. Send it on WhatsApp or upload it in Classroom.
1. What is your bank or team currently ramping up or rolling out? Describe it in 2–3 sentences.
Try to use "to + verb" at least once to explain the purpose.
2. What is one area where you've made real progress this year, and what is one area you still need to address?
Try to use "make real progress in" and "address" from the BBC episode.
3. If you could stand up a new team or process at your bank, what would it be and why?
A good chance to use "stand up," "to + verb," and noun forms.
